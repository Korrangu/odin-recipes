Coconut chutney Recipe

The traditional South Indian side that goes well with almost anything you can think of. Let's see how it's made. The process is really simple.

Ingredients

  • Coconut
  • salt
  • Green Chillies
  • Ginger
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Tamarind
  • Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Bengal gram - 1tsp
  • Mustard seeds - 1tsp
  • dry red chilli - 1
  • cumin seeds - 1tsp
  • asafoetida - 0.5 tsp
  • Curry leaves

The procedure

It's pretty simple. Divide it into two major steps. first being the chutney itself, and the second, it's tempering.

  1. Get the white flesh of a coconut
  2. cut the coconut into small pieces, or you can just grate it
  3. toss them into a blender jar, and add ginger, green chillies, salt, garlic and some tamarind.
  4. Blitz the blender until it's a smooth paste
  5. heat 2 tbsp of oil, and add mustard seeds, the bengal gram, cumin seeds, dry red chilli,asafoetida and curry leaves into the hot oil
  6. transfer your chutney into a serving dish, and add the tempering you just made on top of it

Yep, it's that simple. Super easy and super tasty. You can have it with idli, dosa, pogal, poori or any other breakfast or snack you can think of.
