Idli - An Easy Recipe

The delectable South Indian rice cake is a common breakfast in that region of the country.It's really simple to make, but requires some patience and love to make it right. Let's take a look at it

Ingredients

  • Black lentils (without the skin) - 1 cup
  • Idli Rice (Boiled Rice) - 4 cups
  • Salt - to taste
  • Ghee/butter/oil (for greasing)
  • Love
  • Patience

Apparatus

  • A utensil to soak your seeds in
  • A grinder or a blender (a wet grinder is the best recommendation)
  • A steamer or a pressure cooker
  • Idli moulds/plates

Now that we have all of those, let's begin.

The process

The process starts the night before. Here's an order of the steps.

  1. Soak the lentils and rice spearately for 6-8 hours
  2. Grind each of those into a fine paste, separately while making sure it's not too runny/watery
  3. mix those two batters together and add some salt
  4. Get the steamer going
  5. Grease the moulds/plates and put some batter in it
  6. Steam the batter for 7-9 minutes
  7. Remember, you only steam it. don't pressure cook it.
  8. If using a pressure cooker, don't put the weight/whistle on top and let the steam flow
  9. pull em out and eat em hot! with Chutney or Sambar!
